About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineering graduate with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from ABESIT, AKTU. My journey into tech has been shaped by a passion for building user-centric digital solutions and solving real-world problems using both frontend and backend technologies. I’ve cultivated strong skills in the MERN stack, including React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, along with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also have hands-on experience with RESTful APIs, EJS templating, and development tools like Git, Postman, and VS Code.

During my 8-month internship at Asuun Wellness Pvt. My strength lies in delivering clean, efficient code with attention to UI/UX, responsiveness, and performance.
